How RuralConnect Empowers Villages

Our platform brings together community members and local governance to create more transparent, efficient and participatory village administration.

For Community Members

  • Report local issues like water supply, roads, sanitation
  • Track progress of reported issues through resolution
  • Access village budget information and expenditure details
  • Stay updated on village events and announcements
  • Access important documents and resources

For Village Authorities

  • Manage and respond to community reported issues
  • Publish budget allocations and expenditures transparently
  • Post announcements and upcoming village events
  • Share important documents and resources
  • Document village history and development projects

Budget Transparency

  • View detailed breakdown of village funds allocation
  • Track progress of development projects and their costs
  • See completion status of budgeted projects
  • Access historical budget data and expenditure reports

Issue Resolution Workflow

  • Issues first reviewed by Ward Members for validity
  • Valid issues forwarded to Sarpanch and Upa-Sarpanch
  • Authorities provide updates as issues are addressed
  • Transparent tracking from submission to resolution

Streamlined Issue Resolution

RuralConnect provides a transparent system for reporting and tracking village issues, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.
